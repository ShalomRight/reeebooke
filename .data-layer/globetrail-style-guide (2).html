SURFACES & FILLS

Sections alternate between cream and white. Large filled sections use flat solid blue or pink — no gradients except inside destination circles and stamp image areas.

Cream — page bg
White — section alt
Blue — step cards, hero sections
Pink — popular dest bg, CTA bands
Black — navbar, footer, sidebar
Blue→Pink — destination circles only
Rule: Gradients are only used inside circular destination images and stamp card thumbnail backgrounds — never on section backgrounds or buttons.

BORDERS & SHADOWS

GlobeTrail uses hard offset box-shadows — no blurred drop shadows. Depth is expressed by the shadow offset distance (3, 5, or 8px). All elements carry a solid black border. On hover, elements shift 2px up/left while the shadow grows.

Border weights
3px — Default border (cards, buttons, sections)
2px — Secondary border (inner dividers, inputs)
1.5px — Fine border (badges, chips)
Offset shadows

MOTION

Motion is punchy and mechanical — matching the brutalist aesthetic. The signature interaction is the 2px translate + shadow grow on hover. Nothing floats or fades softly.

Button hover
translate(-2px, -2px)
shadow: +2px offset
All bordered buttons shift up-left on hover; shadow expands. Duration: 120ms.
Destination circle
translate(-2px, -2px)
shadow: sm → md
Circles use the same lift pattern. Creates a "popping off the page" feel.
Stamp card hover
translateY(-4px)
shadow grows
Cards lift slightly more than buttons to signal deeper content.
Transition timing
120–150ms
ease: ease-out
Fast, decisive. Never smooth or floaty — this is a brutalist UI.
Section entrance
fadeIn + Y: 20px → 0
duration: 400ms
Sections animate in on scroll. Staggered by 80ms between items.
Nav search
background fill
shadow: none → sm
Nav items get a cream background fill + border on hover — no colour change.
