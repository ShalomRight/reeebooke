# Design Context: Abby Hair Studio

## Overview
Abby Hair Studio is a Caribbean hair salon in Guyana run by Abby, a solo entrepreneur looking to professionalize her small business. The platform enables clients to discover services, check availability, and book appointments with automated reminders.

---

## Users

### Primary: Salon Clients
- **Who**: Caribbean women seeking natural hair care, locs, styling
- **Context**: Booking on mobile during commutes, lunch breaks, or evenings
- **Job**: Find the right service, see when Abby is available, book without calling
- **Emotions**: Trust, excitement for transformation, relief (easy booking)

### Secondary: Abby (Admin)
- **Who**: Solo salon owner managing her own schedule
- **Context**: Checking appointments between clients, managing availability
- **Job**: See who's coming, manage her calendar, reduce no-shows

---

## Brand Personality

**Three Words**: Warm, Skilled, Unhurried

**Voice**: Like Abby herself — confident but gentle, expert but approachable, Caribbean warmth without being informal. Professional but never cold.

**Emotional Goals**:
- Clients feel *cared for* before they even arrive
- Trust that Abby understands their hair
- Ease of booking reduces anxiety about trying a new stylist

---

## Aesthetic Direction

### Visual Character
**Elegant + Organic + Caribbean Warmth**

- Refined without being sterile (salon ≠ hospital)
- Natural textures and warm tones that evoke Caribbean earth
- Clean layouts that breathe — unhurried, like the service experience
- Mobile-first: most bookings happen on phones

### Theme
**Light mode** with warm undertones. The salon experience is bright, welcoming, and open — not dark or moody.

### References
- Aesop stores: natural materials, warmth, restraint
- Caribbean architecture: terracotta, warm woods, open air
- Editorial beauty layouts: whitespace, product photography

### Anti-References
- Cold clinical blues/grays (medical aesthetic)
- Neon accents or aggressive gradients
- Overly playful/cute (cartoon icons, bubble fonts)
- Generic "AI startup" dark mode with purple accents

---

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth First**: Every interaction should feel like stepping into Abby's welcoming salon. No cold clinical aesthetics.

2. **Mobile is Primary**: The booking flow must be thumb-friendly and work flawlessly on small screens. Touch targets ≥44px.

3. **Trust Through Clarity**: Show availability clearly. No hidden steps. What you see is what you get.

4. **Restrained Elegance**: Use whitespace generously. One beautiful typeface family (system), one accent color, plenty of breathing room.

5. **Caribbean Soul**: Warm earth tones (terracotta, clay, warm sand) that reflect Guyana's natural landscape — never cold corporate blues or generic greens.

---

## Technical

- **Stack**: Next.js 14+, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Typography**: System fonts only (SF Pro, Segoe, Roboto fallback chain)
  - Headings: System sans, light weight (300), tight tracking
  - Body: System sans with monospace accents for metadata
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, focus states visible, reduced motion respected

## Component Patterns

### Cards
- Background: Warm 50 (#FAF9F8)
- Border: 1px solid Warm 200 (#E5E0DC)
- Border-radius: 12px
- Shadow: None (flat, warm aesthetic)
- Padding: 24px

### Buttons
- Primary: Terracotta 800 background, white text
- Secondary: Warm 100 background, Warm 800 text
- Ghost: Transparent, Terracotta 800 text
- Border-radius: 8px (subtle, not pill)

### Inputs
- Background: White
- Border: 1px solid Warm 200
- Focus: Terracotta 400 border
- Border-radius: 8px

---

## Motion Principles

- **Entrance**: Subtle fade-up (y: 20 → 0) with 0.3s ease-out
- **Hover**: Scale 1.02, never bounce or elastic
- **Page transitions**: None (instant for snappy mobile feel)
- **Reduced motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`

---

## Booking Flow UX

1. **Service Selection**: Clear cards with images, prices, duration
2. **Date/Time**: Calendar view optimized for mobile, clear availability
3. **Details**: Minimal form (name, phone, notes)
4. **Confirmation**: Clear summary, add-to-calendar button
5. **Reminders**: WhatsApp/SMS notification at booking + 24h before
